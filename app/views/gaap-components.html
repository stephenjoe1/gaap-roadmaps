Assemble, deploy and run services using common government platforms

Designed with service teams in mind

Service teams doing digital transformation across government try to solve similar issues. Verifying someone’s identity, taking payments or keeping user’s updated about the progress of their case are common elements of many digital transactions.

Common platforms were designed with the needs of these teams in mind:

  • they’re easy to procure, use and integrate with
  • they avoid whenever possible being locked into a vendor
  • they’re supported by the teams that built them
  • their features are based on input from service teams
  • the paid plans are devised to cover costs and not to make a profit

Together with the guidance, standards, patterns and tools provided in the Service Toolkit, they help service teams deliver world-class digital services based on their users’ needs.

Platforms and components

Keep your users updated via emails, text messages and letters with GOV.UK Notify

Take and process payments for your service with GOV.UK Pay
